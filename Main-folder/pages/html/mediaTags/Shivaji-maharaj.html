Information about Shivaji Maharaj

  1. His birth place & date
    • Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19 February 1630.
    • His birth place was Shivneri Fort, Junnar in present day, Maharashtra.
    • His father was Sahaji Bhosale and mother was Jijabai.
  2. Tranning
    • Shivaji Maharaj recieved military tranning under Dadoji Konddev.
    • He was trainedin:
    • Sword Fighting
    • Horse Ridding
    • Archery
    • Guerrilla warfare
    • Fort Management
    • He also learn geograhy of the Sahyadri mountains, which later helped him in warfare.
  3. Top three battles
    • (a) Battle of Pratapghad (1659)
    • Faught between Chhtrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Bijapur general Afzal Khan.
    • Took place near Pratapgad Fort.
    • Shivaji Maharaj used smart military statergy and defat Afzal Khan.
    • This victory increase Shivaji Maharaj fame and strength in Maharashtra.
